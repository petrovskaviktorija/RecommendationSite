About this Recommendation Site

This Recommendation Site is for people who just hate making the decisions. For this reason, the site is created to recommend places to eat/socialize based on the information a user enters. Information would range from the type of meal (date, social gathering, going out with friends), how many people, time constraints, etc. After processing this information the site will recommend a couple of places to eat. This will eliminate the nerves and guesswork when taking out a date that you really like. This will make life much easier for everyone who wants to spend some quality time with food and drink. It is interesting because it will open up new options that may have not been known to exist. Also, it can break a routine that is getting old. Additionally, some feel anxiety when being pressured to make a decision. Finally, this will let you know the wait time at the places the program recommends so you will not be driving all around trying to get a table within a reasonable time frame.
